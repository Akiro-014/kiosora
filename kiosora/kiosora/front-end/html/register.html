Privacy & Security

Privacy Notice

Please read and accept our privacy policy before proceeding.

Data Collection

We collect your student information such as name, birthday, email, address, grade level, and gender solely for managing your student portal account and school records.

Data Protection

Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely in our database. We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Your information is used exclusively for school portal services such as document requests and student record management. We do not share your data with third parties.

Your Rights
