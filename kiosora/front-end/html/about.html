About Us

We present an innovative Smart Kiosk System designed to streamline and modernize document requests within the institution. Our project, Kiosora, was created to streamline document requests and reduce congestion in registrar offices. Through innovation and user-centered design, we aim to make academic services faster, more accessible, and more efficient.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make document requests faster, easier, and more accessible for everyone. Through our Automated Kiosk System with seamless printing functionality, we simplify the entire process into a smooth and user-friendly experience. We strive to reduce waiting time, eliminate unnecessary steps, and provide a reliable and hassle-free service that meets the needs of every user.
